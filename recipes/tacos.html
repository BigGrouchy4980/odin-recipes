The Best Taco Recipe

Ingredients needed:

  • Ground beef
  • (1) pack of taco seasoning
  • Taco shells (hard or soft)
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Taco sauce (mild or hot)
  • Onions (optional)

How to cook:

  1. Cook ground beef
  2. Add in taco seasoning and cook per package instructions
  3. Heat up taco shells per package instructions
  4. Assemble your taco
  5. Enjoy!
